1. Data Collection

In the past, third-party cookies tracked you across the web to build a perfect profile. Today, platforms rely heavily on First-Party Data (what you do directly on their app/site) and Contextual signals (the topic of the page you are on).

🛡️

2. The Privacy Impact

Your consent choices legally block data. If you disable cross-site tracking or location sharing, the algorithm's vision becomes blurred. It cannot use your search history or actual interests to pick the ad.

⚖️

3. The Auction

Ads are scored based on Relevance (matching your known signals) and Bid (how much the advertiser pays). With limited data, broad contextual ads often outscore high-bid, hyper-targeted ads.
